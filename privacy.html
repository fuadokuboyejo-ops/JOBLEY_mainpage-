PRIVACY POLICY

LAST UPDATED: FEBRUARY 6, 2026

01

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information – Email address, name, and authentication credentials.
  • Profile Data – Resume, skills, desired professions, availability, and location preferences.
  • Application Data – Job applications you create, including company names, roles, and application status.
  • Usage Data – How you interact with the app, including swipes, searches, and feature usage.
  • Device Information – Device type, operating system version, and app version.
02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized job recommendations based on your profile.
  • Track your job applications and provide status updates.
  • Improve our AI-powered matching algorithms.
  • Analyze app performance and user experience.
  • Communicate important updates and new features.
  • Provide customer support.
03

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Encryption: Resume data is encrypted using iOS Keychain.
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Firebase Auth.
  • Database: Data stored in Supabase with row-level security.
  • Local Storage: Sensitive data never stored in plain text.
  • SSL/TLS: All network communications are encrypted.
04

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication – For secure user authentication.
  • Supabase – For database and backend services.
  • Mistral AI – For AI-powered resume grading and interview prep.

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

05

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction – Update or correct your information.
  • Deletion – Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Export – Download your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out – Disable certain data collection features.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@jobley.app.

06

Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Active accounts: Data retained while your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Backups: May remain in backups for up to 90 days.
  • Legal requirements: May retain data longer if required by law.
07

Children's Privacy

Jobley is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Displaying a notice in the app.
  • Sending an email notification.
  • Updating the "Last updated" date.

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

09

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:


✉ support@jobley.app

Website: jobley.app

We will respond to your inquiry within 48 hours.
