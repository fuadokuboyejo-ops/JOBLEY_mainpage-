Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jobley?

Jobley is an iOS app designed to help job seekers organize and track their job applications. Keep all your applications, interviews, and deadlines in one place with beautiful visualizations of your job search journey.

How do I get started?

Simply download Jobley from the App Store, create an account, and start adding your job applications. The intuitive interface will guide you through organizing your job search.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I report a bug or issue?

If you encounter any issues, please email us at jobley64@gmail.com with a detailed description of the problem. Include your device model and iOS version if possible.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to jobley64@gmail.com with your account email. We'll process your request within 48 hours.
